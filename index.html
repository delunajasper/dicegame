Game Rules

  • The game has 2 players, playing in rounds
  • A player rolls a dice as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to his score
  • The player can choose to 'Hold Dice', which means that his score gets added to his dom. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • If the player rolls a 1, all his score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • Player loses 40 points and turn if double 6 is rolled.
  • First player to reach 100 point wins. They can set the winning goal score below.
